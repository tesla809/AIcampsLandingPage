About

AI Camp is an open source, community-run and not-for-profit conference focused on open source artificial intelligence (AI), deep learning and machine learning technologies.

AI Camp will involve a smaller daytime event followed by a large cross-community meetup during the evening, all hosted at the United Nations HQ in NYC

Connect with the best minds in the artificial intelligence community to share ideas and brainstorm new solutions to the world's most challenging problems at the heart of the UN.

Hear industry leaders talk about best practices, comming innovations, and social effects.

Learn about the latest research and technologies that you can use immediately to invent the future.
